About

NEX, or NotExpired checks meats and other food items for their 'true' expiration date.

How to use

  1. Create or download a database in the settings menu.
  2. Select your item from the database. Have your printed expiration date ready.
  3. Type in the printed expiration date.
  4. Ta-da, you have your 'true' expiration date of your item!
